# Ontario Mushroom Competitors: Growers and Grower-Distributors

Checked on April 12, 2026 using supplier public websites. Expanded on April 28, 2026 with farm-led leads found during distributor research, especially near Brantford. Certification column added on April 28, 2026 using the CanadaGAP certified-company list, SQFI public directory records where searchable, and supplier public pages.

This file contains Ontario mushroom farms and grower-distributors that are more likely competitors than direct buyer leads for a shiitake and oyster mushroom farm.

## Type Legend

- `Grower / farm`: Primarily grows its own mushrooms and sells direct or through partners.
- `Grower / farm + distributor`: Grows mushrooms but also operates broader wholesale or distribution channels.

## Notes

- Ravine, Guan's, and Pfenning's remain in this competitor file even though they have distribution elements, because they are farm-led businesses.
- Holburne and WoodBloom both mention distributor partners, so they may still be useful for channel intelligence even though they are competitors.
- Brantford Mushroom Farm and Nature Lion are included because they are near Brantford and can confuse distributor research; they should not be treated as fresh mushroom buyer leads without direct evidence that they buy from outside growers.
- Pfenning's is not a verified mushroom grower from the public pages checked, but it was moved here from `distributors.md` because it is clearly farm-led.
- If a public site did not explicitly list a mushroom type, it is not marked as verified here.
- "No public CanadaGAP or SQF evidence found" means the business did not appear under the checked name variants in the April 2026 CanadaGAP certified-company list, the public SQFI directory/search results checked, or the supplier pages checked. Treat this as a public-evidence finding, not a definitive legal certification opinion.
- CanadaGAP findings used the [CanadaGAP certified-company list](https://www.canadagap.ca/certification/certified-companies/), which states that certification bodies remain the definitive source for current status.
- SQF findings used the [SQFI certified site directory](https://directory.sqfi.com/search-for-sqf-certified-supplier/) where searchable, plus supplier pages for Holburne, Carleton, Ravine, Enviro, and Nebo.
