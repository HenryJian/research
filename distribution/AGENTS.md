# Distribution Directory Map

- [`competitors.md`](competitors.md): Research results about competing farms and suppliers.
- [`distributors.md`](distributors.md): Research results about distributors and wholesalers that may be potential buyers or channel partners.
  - Note, this page should not contain shiitake farms or growers, because they're our competitors and they will not buy from us; for those farms or growers, put them in the [`competitors.md`](competitors.md).
- [`restaurants.md`](restaurants.md): Research results about restaurants that have shiitake / oyster / maitake on their menu. These are the end customers
- [`restaurant-menus`](restaurant-menus): Saved menu pages and related source material used to verify restaurant-side research.
