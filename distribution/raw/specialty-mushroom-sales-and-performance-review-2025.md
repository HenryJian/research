### Specialty Mushroom Share of Market / 特种蘑菇市场份额

Specialty mushrooms represented 7.0% of total fresh mushroom dollar sales in retail channels excluding ethnic or organic stores in the past year ("traditional retail"). / 在过去一年中，特种蘑菇在剔除族裔超市和有机门店后的零售渠道中，占鲜蘑菇总销售额的 7.0%，这里称为“传统零售”。

The Circana MULO+ universe does not include Asian store formats such as H-Mart and Lotte, nor Whole Foods and Sprouts, which all have a large presence of specialty mushrooms compared to most traditional supermarkets, supercenters, clubs, etc. / Circana MULO+ 的覆盖范围不包括 H-Mart、Lotte 这样的亚洲连锁门店，也不包括 Whole Foods 和 Sprouts；相比大多数传统超市、大卖场和仓储会员店，这些渠道中的特种蘑菇占比通常更高。

The specialty mushroom share of business in traditional retail is down from 7.2% the prior year but up from 6.6% two years ago. / 特种蘑菇在传统零售中的销售份额较上一年的 7.2% 有所下降，但高于两年前的 6.6%。

The unit (3.8%) and volume/pound (2.2%) shares are far smaller. Both declined a little bit from their 2024 levels. / 按件数计算的份额为 3.8%，按重量计算的份额为 2.2%，都明显低于销售额份额，而且两项都较 2024 年略有下降。

Specialty mushrooms generated $102 million in the latest 52 weeks. Dollars, units and pounds are trending down compared to last year's levels. / 在最近 52 周中，特种蘑菇创造了 1.02 亿美元销售额。销售额、件数和重量都低于上一年水平。

While white and brown mushrooms are experiencing year-over-year decreases as well, specialty mushroom sales are declining at a greater rate. / 尽管白蘑菇和褐蘑菇也在同比下滑，但特种蘑菇的下降幅度更大。

### Understanding the Impact of Distribution (MULO+ | 52 weeks ending 11/2/2025) / 理解铺货分销的影响（MULO+ | 截至 2025-11-02 的 52 周）

- Distribution is highest and mostly unchanged for white and crimini mushrooms. / 白蘑菇和 crimini 蘑菇的铺货分销率最高，且整体变化不大。
- Portabella mushrooms have distribution in less than two-thirds of stores and experienced a small decline. / portabella 蘑菇的铺货门店比例低于三分之二，并且出现小幅下降。
- In total, specialty mushrooms have distribution in a little more than 40% of the traditional grocery channels (excluding ethnic formats). / 总体来看，特种蘑菇在传统食品零售渠道中的铺货覆盖率略高于 40%（不含族裔门店）。
- This is down by nearly three percentage points compared to last year, after peaking in 2023. / 在 2023 年达到高点之后，这一覆盖率较去年下降了接近 3 个百分点。
- Among the specialty varieties, shiitake have the highest distribution, though lost two percentage points year-over-year. / 在特种蘑菇中，香菇的铺货率最高，但同比下降了 2 个百分点。
- Oyster mushrooms have the second-widest distribution that peaked in 2024. / 平菇的铺货广度位居第二，并在 2024 年达到峰值。
- All other varieties have distribution of less than 5%. While growing, this growth is off a very small base. / 其他品类的铺货率都低于 5%。虽然其中一些在增长，但都是在很小的基数上增长。

### Pricing and Promoting (MULO+ | 52 weeks ending 11/2/2025) / 定价与促销（MULO+ | 截至 2025-11-02 的 52 周）

### Takeaways Specialty Mushrooms / 特种蘑菇要点总结

1. Specialty mushrooms are a niche offering in traditional food retail outlets and losing some momentum. / 特种蘑菇在传统食品零售渠道中仍属于小众品类，而且正在失去一部分增长动能。

2. Dollars, units, and volume sales are all declining. The segment is not keeping pace with the broader mushroom category and is disproportionately weighted toward one variety. / 销售额、件数和重量销量都在下降。这个细分品类没有跟上整体蘑菇大类的节奏，而且高度集中在单一品种上。

3. Shiitake dominates the segment, but sales are soft. The top sellers list reflects reliance on sliced and small pack-size shiitake. Most of the other larger specialty varieties, including mixed, oyster, and chanterelle, also saw declines. / 香菇主导了这个细分市场，但销售表现偏弱。畅销单品榜显示该品类高度依赖切片和小包装香菇。其他较大的特种品类，包括混合蘑菇、平菇和鸡油菌，也大多出现下滑。

4. The growth is concentrated in small, emerging varieties, including lion's mane, trumpet, beech and enoki, but all are gains off very small bases and impacted by distribution growth. / 增长主要集中在较小、较新的品类上，包括猴头菇、trumpet 菇、beech 菇和金针菇，但这些增长都是建立在很小的基数之上，而且明显受铺货增长推动。

5. Specialty mushroom distribution is far below that of whites and browns. Shiitake and oyster have the highest distribution among specialties, while all others remain under 5%, despite some growing from tiny bases. Distribution experienced a 3-point drop in stores carrying specialties and limited placement outside coastal/urban regions suggests that retail availability could be an opportunity in areas that are a demographic match for specialty mushroom shoppers (younger, high-income foodies). / 特种蘑菇的铺货率远低于白蘑菇和褐蘑菇。香菇和平菇在特种蘑菇中铺货率最高，而其他品类即便有所增长，铺货率仍都低于 5%。经营特种蘑菇的门店覆盖率下降了 3 个百分点，而在沿海和城市地区之外铺货有限，这说明对于与特种蘑菇消费人群画像相符的区域（更年轻、高收入、偏美食爱好者），零售可得性仍是机会点。

6. Premium pricing remains stable, with little promotion given the foodie nature of the category. At the same time, the high(er) price per unit may limit trial in a value-driven environment. Combined with minimal merchandising support, this reinforces the category's niche status. / 溢价定价整体保持稳定。由于该品类偏向美食型消费，促销投入很少。与此同时，在当前更注重性价比的环境中，较高的单件价格可能会限制新客尝试，再加上陈列支持有限，这进一步强化了该品类的小众属性。

7. Regional performance is uneven. The Northeast is the biggest specialty region and over-indexes in share, but California is the only region showing dollar and unit growth. / 区域表现并不均衡。东北部是最大的特种蘑菇市场，且份额高于平均，但只有加利福尼亚同时实现了销售额和件数增长。

### What this means / 这意味着什么

- Reversing distribution losses is essential. This is the biggest controllable lever for stabilizing specialty performance. / 扭转铺货下滑至关重要。这是稳定特种蘑菇表现的最大可控杠杆。
- Broader promotion and education (usage ideas, nutrition messaging and preparation inspiration) may help close the trial gap. / 更广泛的促销和消费者教育（如食用方法、营养信息和烹饪灵感）可能有助于缩小尝试门槛。
- Innovation and assortment optimization could emphasize growing varieties such as lion's mane, enoki and trumpet. / 产品创新和品类优化可以更多强调正在增长的品种，例如猴头菇、金针菇和 trumpet 菇。
- Right-sizing shiitake and mixed offerings may be necessary given some sales softness. / 考虑到销售疲软，香菇和混合蘑菇产品的规格与组合可能需要重新调整。
- Targeted regional strategies (especially in California and the Northeast) can accelerate gains where interest is highest. / 有针对性的区域策略，尤其是在加利福尼亚和东北部，可以在需求兴趣最高的地区加快增长。
