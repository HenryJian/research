# Holburne Mushroom Farm Retail Footprint

Checked on 2026-04-16.

## Bottom Line

- Holburne publicly says its mushrooms are sold to retail and foodservice customers across Canada, reaching as far west as Vancouver and as far east as Quebec.
- I also found a newer industry interview, published on 2025-02-27, where co-owner Steven Rotiroti says Holburne ships across Canada and into the U.S. through a third-party company.
- I found public, named grocery-market evidence for Ontario retailers, but I did **not** find public pages naming Holburne-branded supermarket chains in British Columbia or Quebec.

## Coverage Evidence

| Question | Answer | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Do they sell in Canada? | Yes. | Holburne's Foodservice & Retail page says the farm supplies retailers and foodservice across Canada, with western reach to Vancouver and eastern reach to Quebec. Source: [Holburne - Foodservice & Retail](https://www.holburnemushroom.ca/foodservice-retail) |
| Do they sell on Canada's west coast? | Yes, publicly claimed. | Holburne's own retail page says distribution goes as far west as Vancouver. Source: [Holburne - Foodservice & Retail](https://www.holburnemushroom.ca/foodservice-retail) |
| Do they sell in the U.S.? | Likely yes, but the public evidence is weaker than for Canada. | In a 2025-02-27 FreshPlaza interview, Steven Rotiroti said the company ships across Canada and into the U.S. through a third-party company. Source: [FreshPlaza - Demand picks up for exotic mushrooms in Canada](https://www.freshplaza.com/north-america/article/9708593/demand-picks-up-for-exotic-mushrooms-in-canada/) |

## What I Did Not Confirm

- I did not find a public list from Holburne naming all distributor partners or supermarket accounts.
- I did not find named Holburne-branded supermarket chains in British Columbia, Alberta, Manitoba, Saskatchewan, or Quebec, despite Holburne's Canada-wide coverage claim.
- I did not find a public U.S. retailer list for Holburne-branded mushrooms. The U.S. evidence currently comes from the FreshPlaza interview, not from a named U.S. supermarket page.

## Practical Read

- Holburne clearly has a Canada-wide wholesale / retail distribution footprint.
- Ontario is the easiest province to verify with named retail evidence.
- West-coast Canada appears to be real distribution coverage, but not publicly mapped to named supermarket chains.
- U.S. sales also appear to exist, but the public evidence suggests they may be routed through an intermediary rather than through clearly named direct supermarket placements.
