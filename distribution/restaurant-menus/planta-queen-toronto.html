PLANTA Queen Toronto is the local favorite for the best vegan sushi and wok specialties! Drop in for plant-powered dinner, brunch, lunch and happy hour near Queen Street, King Street, Four Seasons Performing Arts, YukYuk Comedy Club, and more!

FAQ

Is PLANTA Queen Toronto a vegan restaurant?

Yes, PLANTA Queen in Toronto is a 100% plant-based vegan restaurant offering innovative Asian-inspired cuisine.

What makes PLANTA Queen Toronto a top plant-based restaurant?

Our Toronto location features a creative plant-based menu with standout dishes like vegan sushi, nigiri, handrolls, handhelds, pasta, and seasonal bowls—all served in a vibrant, sustainable setting.

Does PLANTA Queen Toronto offer gluten-free vegan options?

Yes, we offer many gluten-free and vegan options at our Toronto location, including gluten-free sushi rolls and 'Chilled & Raw' items. Ask your server for guidance.

What are the most popular dishes at PLANTA Queen Toronto?

Guest favorites include our Bang Bang Broccoli, Udon Noodles, Spicy Tuna Roll, PLANTA Burger, Unagi Eggplant Nigiri, and Beef & Broccoli. 

What plant-based desserts are on the menu?

Our menu features delicious plant-based desserts perfect for anyone seeking vegan and dairy-free sweet treats. You can enjoy our Matcha Cheesecake, which is gluten-free and topped with a tangy berry compote and creamy coconut whip. Another favorite is our Banana Pudding, made with rich banana custard, coconut caramel, dairy-free whipped cream, vanilla cake, and chocolate shavings.

Where in PLANTA Queen Toronto located?

We’re at 180 Queen St W, Toronto, ON M5V 3X3, Canada. Our cross streets are Queen St W and St Patrick St.

Where can I park when visiting PLANTA Queen Toronto?

The closest parking is located at 35 Simco Street in the Sun Life building. 

Can non-vegans enjoy PLANTA Queen Toronto?

Absolutely. PLANTA Queen Toronto is designed for everyone—from committed vegans to curious food lovers. Our menu focuses on bold flavors, creative textures, and beautiful presentation that appeals to plant-based and non-plant-based guests alike. Many first-time visitors are surprised by how satisfying and delicious plant-based dining can be—even without traditional meat or dairy.

Does PLANTA Queen Toronto have good vegan food for date night?

Yes! PLANTA is one of the best spots for a vegan date night in Toronto. With moody lighting, beautifully designed interiors, and an elevated plant-based menu that includes dishes like truffle sushi, dumplings, and handcrafted cocktails, PLANTA Queen offers a vibe that’s both intimate and impressive—perfect for a night out.

What vegan options are kid-friendly at PLANTA Queen Toronto?

PLANTA Queen Toronto offers a dedicated kids’ menu designed to make plant-based dining fun and approachable for younger guests. With dishes that are simple, flavorful, and 100% vegan, even picky eaters will find something to love. Kid-friendly favorites include the Avocado Cucumber Roll, Fried Rice, Udon Noodles, and Watermelon Juice—all crafted with the same quality and care as our full menu.
