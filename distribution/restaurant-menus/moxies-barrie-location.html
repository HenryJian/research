Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, you can make a reservation online here, or by calling the restaurant directly.

Where can I find nutrition and allergy information?

You can view nutritional information on our Menu page. Please advise your server about food sensitivities & ensure you speak to a manager regarding severe allergies.

Do you sell restaurant gift cards and how can I purchase one?

You can purchase a gift card in store, or by visiting our Gift Card page or Corporate Gift Card page

Do you have a Kid's Menu?

Yes, we do! You can view our Kids’ Menu here.

Is your restaurant child friendly?

Absolutely! Our dining rooms are child and family friendly. We welcome families with children of all ages and offer a Kids’ Menu that includes a drinks and dessert for children under 12. We also offer colouring sheets with crayons and have high chairs available.

Can I watch the game at your restaurant?

Yes, our locations have TV screens for watching live sports and events.

Do you have birthdays specials?

Yes, guests celebrating their birthday at Moxies receive a complimentary treat. Please let your server know if someone in your party is celebrating.

Do you do delivery or take-out?

Yes. Order Moxies delivery or takeout online here.

How do I apply for a job?

You can view current job opening and apply online here.

Do you have a Happy Hour, and what time is it?

Yes. Moxies offers Happy Hour twice daily, every day. Select your preferred location on the Locations page to view current Happy Hour times and menus.

Do you have daily specials?

Yes, we offer daily drink specials. Visit our menu page and select the Drink menu to view today’s specials.

Do you take bookings or reservations for large groups?

Yes. Moxies is the perfect venue for group dining and events. Visit our Group Bookings page and select your preferred location for details or to submit a booking inquiry.

Is your restaurant dog friendly?

Our restaurants follow all provincial regulations regarding dogs on patios and restaurant premises. Please contact your preferred Moxies location directly for more information.

Do you have wheelchair accessibility?

Yes, all Moxies locations are wheelchair accessible. Please contact your preferred Moxies location directly if you have any questions or concerns about accessibility.

Do you have parking?

Parking varies by location. Please contact your preferred Moxies location directly if you have any questions or concerns about parking access.

Does your restaurant have gluten conscious or vegetarian items on the menu?

Yes, our menu features gluten-conscious and vegetarian options. Gluten-conscious items are marked with (GC), and vegetarian options are marked with (V) for vegetarian. Some dishes are also fully plant-based or can be made plant-based. Please inform your server of any dietary requirements or allergies before ordering.

Do you have patio seating?

As each Moxies location is unique, patio availability and setup vary by restaurant. Please contact your preferred Moxies location directly to learn more about patio seating and outdoor dining options.

Do you have a private dining room?

Private dining options vary by location. Many Moxies restaurants offer private or semi-private spaces that are perfect for celebrations, corporate events, and special occasions. Room size and availability differ by restaurant, so please visit our Group Bookings page and select your location for more details, or contact your preferred Moxies location directly.

Do you have non-alcoholic/zero-proof offering?

Yes, we offer non-alcoholic beer and a variety of zero-proof cocktails. View all spirit-free options on our menu. 

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, we welcome walk-ins. Availability can vary and wait times may occur during busy periods. We recommend making a reservation to ensure your preferred dining time.
