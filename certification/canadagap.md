# CanadaGAP certification for a shiitake farm
# 香菇农场的 CanadaGAP 认证

Research date: 2026-04-28  
研究日期：2026-04-28

Farm context: specialty mushroom farm in Waterford, Ontario; current crop is shiitake.  
农场背景：位于安大略省 Waterford 的特色蘑菇农场；当前作物为香菇。

## Bottom line
## 结论

CanadaGAP is not currently a usable certification path for certifying shiitake growing, harvesting, first packing, or storage. The 2026 CanadaGAP Program Management Manual says certification is not available for production, packing, and storage of products outside program scope, and gives "mushrooms" as an example of an outside-scope product. The 2026 fruit/vegetable and greenhouse manuals list the crops eligible for production/packing/storage; shiitake and other mushrooms are not listed.

CanadaGAP 目前不是一条可用于认证香菇种植、采收、首次包装或储存的认证路径。2026 年 CanadaGAP Program Management Manual 说明，对于不在项目范围内的产品，其生产、包装和储存不能获得认证，并把 "mushrooms"（蘑菇）列为范围外产品的例子。2026 年水果/蔬菜手册和温室产品手册列出了可用于生产/包装/储存认证的作物；香菇和其他蘑菇不在清单中。

CanadaGAP may still be relevant only if the business operates as a repacker, wholesaler, or broker of already market-ready fresh mushrooms, not as the producer/first packer of its own shiitake. Even then, enoki mushrooms are excluded from repacking, and the certificate would cover the repacking/wholesaling/brokerage process, not mushroom production.

只有在企业作为已经可上市鲜蘑菇的再包装商、批发商或经纪商运营时，CanadaGAP 才可能仍然相关；这不适用于认证我们自有香菇的生产或首次包装。即便如此，金针菇也被排除在再包装范围之外，而且证书覆盖的是再包装/批发/经纪流程，而不是蘑菇生产本身。

Operational recommendation: do not spend money trying to enroll our shiitake production under CanadaGAP unless CanadaGAP or a Certification Body gives written scope confirmation for our exact proposed activity. If a buyer asks for CanadaGAP on shiitake, the practical response is to explain that CanadaGAP does not cover mushroom production and ask what alternate food-safety certification or buyer audit they will accept.

运营建议：除非 CanadaGAP 或某个认证机构针对我们拟开展的具体活动提供书面范围确认，否则不要花钱尝试把我们的香菇生产纳入 CanadaGAP。如果买家要求香菇具备 CanadaGAP，实际回应应是说明 CanadaGAP 不覆盖蘑菇生产，并询问他们接受哪种替代食品安全认证或买家审核。

## Why shiitake production is outside CanadaGAP scope
## 为什么香菇生产不在 CanadaGAP 范围内

CanadaGAP is a food-safety program for companies that produce, handle, and broker fruits and vegetables. Its certification audits assess whether a company maintains the CanadaGAP food safety system; the Certification Body certifies processes, not the product itself. Source: [CanadaGAP overview](https://www.canadagap.ca/program/).

CanadaGAP 是面向水果和蔬菜生产、处理及经纪企业的食品安全项目。其认证审核评估企业是否维持 CanadaGAP 食品安全体系；认证机构认证的是流程，而不是产品本身。来源：[CanadaGAP overview](https://www.canadagap.ca/program/)。

For production, packing, and storage, CanadaGAP certificates must identify individual crops, and the crop can only be one listed in the CanadaGAP Food Safety Manual for Fresh Fruits and Vegetables or the CanadaGAP Food Safety Manual for Greenhouse Product. Source: [CanadaGAP Program Management Manual v11.0, 2026](https://www.canadagap.ca/uploads/288/canadagap-program/22235/component-3-canadagap-program-management-manual-v11-0-2026-en-uncontrolled-copy.pdf).

对于生产、包装和储存，CanadaGAP 证书必须列明具体作物，而且该作物只能是 CanadaGAP Fresh Fruits and Vegetables Food Safety Manual 或 CanadaGAP Greenhouse Product Food Safety Manual 中列出的作物。来源：[CanadaGAP Program Management Manual v11.0, 2026](https://www.canadagap.ca/uploads/288/canadagap-program/22235/component-3-canadagap-program-management-manual-v11-0-2026-en-uncontrolled-copy.pdf)。

The current manuals do not list mushrooms as eligible production/packing/storage crops:

当前手册没有把蘑菇列为可用于生产/包装/储存认证的作物：

- [Fruit and Vegetable Manual v11.0, effective April 1, 2026](https://www.canadagap.ca/uploads/447/version-11-0/22046/canadagap-fruit-and-vegetable-manual-11-0-2026-eng-fillable.pdf): covers field/orchard/vineyard-grown combined vegetables, potatoes, leafy vegetables and cruciferae, small fruit, and tree/vine fruit. Mushrooms are not in the crop list.
- [Fruit and Vegetable Manual v11.0, 2026 年 4 月 1 日生效](https://www.canadagap.ca/uploads/447/version-11-0/22046/canadagap-fruit-and-vegetable-manual-11-0-2026-eng-fillable.pdf)：覆盖田间/果园/葡萄园种植的综合蔬菜、马铃薯、叶菜和十字花科蔬菜、小果类，以及树生/藤生水果。蘑菇不在作物清单中。
- [Greenhouse Product Manual v11.0, effective April 1, 2026](https://www.canadagap.ca/uploads/447/version-11-0/22049/canadagap-greenhouse-manual-11-0-2026-eng-fillable.pdf): covers greenhouse combined vegetables, leafy vegetables and cruciferae, and small fruit. Mushrooms are not in the crop list.
- [Greenhouse Product Manual v11.0, 2026 年 4 月 1 日生效](https://www.canadagap.ca/uploads/447/version-11-0/22049/canadagap-greenhouse-manual-11-0-2026-eng-fillable.pdf)：覆盖温室综合蔬菜、叶菜和十字花科蔬菜，以及小果类。蘑菇不在作物清单中。

The Program Management Manual is more explicit: CanadaGAP certification is not available for production, packing, and storage of products outside the program scope, including "mushrooms." It also excludes wild-gathered products such as wild mushrooms. Source: [CanadaGAP Program Management Manual v11.0, section 3.3.5.2](https://www.canadagap.ca/certification/management-manual/).

Program Management Manual 更明确：对于项目范围之外的产品，包括 "mushrooms"（蘑菇），CanadaGAP 不提供其生产、包装和储存认证。它也排除野外采集产品，例如野生蘑菇。来源：[CanadaGAP Program Management Manual v11.0, section 3.3.5.2](https://www.canadagap.ca/certification/management-manual/)。

## What CanadaGAP can cover around mushrooms
## CanadaGAP 在蘑菇相关业务中可能覆盖什么

CanadaGAP's repacking, wholesaling, and brokerage scope is broader than its production scope. The fruit/vegetable and greenhouse manuals both state that they cover repacking, wholesaling, and brokerage of fresh fruits and vegetables except:

CanadaGAP 的再包装、批发和经纪范围比其生产范围更宽。水果/蔬菜手册和温室产品手册均说明，它们覆盖鲜果蔬的再包装、批发和经纪，但以下情况除外：

- fresh sprouts,
- 新鲜芽菜，
- repacking of enoki mushrooms,
- 金针菇再包装，
- fresh fruits and vegetables in hermetically sealed containers,
- 密封容器中的新鲜水果和蔬菜，
- minimally processed fruits and vegetables.
- 最低限度加工的水果和蔬菜。

The manuals also include a mushroom-specific rule for "mushrooms for repacking only (except enoki)": if non-perforated plastic film is used, perforations must be added. That rule points to Health Canada's packaging guidance for fresh mushrooms. Source: [Health Canada mushroom packaging guidance](https://www.canada.ca/en/health-canada/services/food-nutrition/legislation-guidelines/guidance-documents/guidance-concerning-packaging-fresh-mushrooms.html).

手册还包含一条针对蘑菇的规则，适用于 "mushrooms for repacking only (except enoki)"（仅用于再包装的蘑菇，金针菇除外）：如果使用未打孔塑料膜，则必须增加孔洞。该规则指向加拿大卫生部关于鲜蘑菇包装的指导。来源：[Health Canada mushroom packaging guidance](https://www.canada.ca/en/health-canada/services/food-nutrition/legislation-guidelines/guidance-documents/guidance-concerning-packaging-fresh-mushrooms.html)。

Practical interpretation for us:

对我们的实际解释：

- Growing shiitake, harvesting shiitake, placing harvested shiitake into the first market-ready package, and storing our own harvested/packed shiitake are not CanadaGAP-certifiable activities.
- 种植香菇、采收香菇、把采收后的香菇放入首次可上市包装，以及储存我们自己采收/包装的香菇，都不是可通过 CanadaGAP 认证的活动。
- Buying or receiving already market-ready mushrooms, removing them from market-ready packaging, sorting/re-grading/re-trimming/re-washing as applicable, and repacking them may be certifiable as repacking, except for enoki.
- 购买或接收已经可上市的蘑菇，将其从可上市包装中取出，并按需要进行分拣/重新分级/重新修整/重新清洗后再包装，可能可作为再包装活动认证，但金针菇除外。
- Storing and selling already market-ready mushrooms without repacking may be certifiable as wholesaling.
- 在不再包装的情况下储存并销售已经可上市的蘑菇，可能可作为批发活动认证。
- Arranging sales without physically handling product may be certifiable as brokerage.
- 在不实际接触产品的情况下安排销售，可能可作为经纪活动认证。
- A repacking/wholesale/brokerage certificate would not make claims about how the mushrooms were grown.
- 再包装/批发/经纪证书不会对蘑菇如何种植作出声明。

## If pursuing the limited repacking/wholesale route
## 如果考虑有限的再包装/批发路径

Only pursue this if a buyer will accept a certificate that covers repacking/wholesaling/brokerage rather than production. The likely routes are:

只有在买家愿意接受覆盖再包装/批发/经纪、而非生产的证书时，才考虑这条路径。可能的路径包括：

- **Option D**: annual on-site audit for repacking or wholesaling; GFSI-recognized.
- **Option D**：针对再包装或批发的年度现场审核；获得 GFSI 认可。
- **Option F**: annual on-site audit for repacking, wholesaling, or brokerage; not GFSI-recognized.
- **Option F**：针对再包装、批发或经纪的年度现场审核；不获得 GFSI 认可。

What we would need to do:

我们需要做的事项：

1. Get written scope confirmation from CanadaGAP or a licensed Certification Body for the exact mushroom activity. Describe whether product is our own crop or incoming market product, whether we remove product from market-ready packaging, whether we store product, and whether we broker without handling.
   中文：向 CanadaGAP 或持牌认证机构取得针对具体蘑菇活动的书面范围确认。说明产品是我们自己的作物还是外来的市场产品，是否会从可上市包装中取出产品，是否储存产品，以及是否在不接触产品的情况下进行经纪。
2. Choose a Certification Body. CanadaGAP currently lists BNQ, Control Union, MSVS, NSF Canada Ag, and TSLC as licensed Certification Bodies. Ask each for Ontario auditor availability, audit rate, travel estimate, and whether they will certify the proposed mushroom repacking/wholesale scope. Source: [CanadaGAP Certification Bodies](https://www.canadagap.ca/certification/certification-bodies/).
   中文：选择认证机构。CanadaGAP 目前列出 BNQ、Control Union、MSVS、NSF Canada Ag 和 TSLC 为持牌认证机构。向每家询问安大略审核员可用性、审核费率、差旅估算，以及他们是否会认证拟议的蘑菇再包装/批发范围。来源：[CanadaGAP Certification Bodies](https://www.canadagap.ca/certification/certification-bodies/)。
3. Enroll with CanadaGAP using the enrolment form and selected certification option. Source: [CanadaGAP enrolment form](https://www.canadagap.ca/certification/enrolment-form/).
   中文：使用登记表和所选认证选项向 CanadaGAP 登记。来源：[CanadaGAP enrolment form](https://www.canadagap.ca/certification/enrolment-form/)。
4. Implement the applicable CanadaGAP manual sections and use the current Audit Checklist before the audit. The 2026 manuals and Audit Checklist v11.0 took effect April 1, 2026. Sources: [Manual downloads](https://www.canadagap.ca/manuals/downloads/) and [Audit Checklist](https://www.canadagap.ca/audit-checklist/).
   中文：在审核前实施适用的 CanadaGAP 手册章节，并使用当前 Audit Checklist。2026 年手册和 Audit Checklist v11.0 于 2026 年 4 月 1 日生效。来源：[Manual downloads](https://www.canadagap.ca/manuals/downloads/) 和 [Audit Checklist](https://www.canadagap.ca/audit-checklist/)。
5. Develop and maintain a site-specific HACCP plan. CanadaGAP requires this for repacking and wholesaling operations.
   中文：制定并维护场地专属 HACCP 计划。CanadaGAP 要求再包装和批发业务具备该计划。
6. Keep at least three months of records before the initial audit. Keep manual sections, forms, receipts, letters of assurance, and certificates for at least four years.
   中文：首次审核前至少保留三个月记录。手册章节、表格、收据、保证函和证书至少保留四年。
7. Complete an annual review and internal audit using the CanadaGAP Self-Assessment Checklist or Audit Checklist.
   中文：使用 CanadaGAP Self-Assessment Checklist 或 Audit Checklist 完成年度审查和内部审核。
8. Schedule the audit while the relevant activity is occurring. CanadaGAP says audits must occur during harvest, handling, packing/repacking season, shipping, or other relevant activities; activities not occurring cannot be included in scope. Repacking and wholesaling audits take at least one day. Source: [CanadaGAP audit process FAQ](https://www.canadagap.ca/program/faq/audit-process/).
   中文：在相关活动正在进行时安排审核。CanadaGAP 表示，审核必须在采收、处理、包装/再包装季、发货或其他相关活动期间进行；未发生的活动不能纳入范围。再包装和批发审核至少需要一天。来源：[CanadaGAP audit process FAQ](https://www.canadagap.ca/program/faq/audit-process/)。
9. After the audit, the auditor reports to the Certification Body, and the Certification Body decides whether to issue the certificate. Source: [Audit and certification process](https://www.canadagap.ca/certification/audit-and-certification-process/).
   中文：审核后，审核员向认证机构报告，由认证机构决定是否签发证书。来源：[Audit and certification process](https://www.canadagap.ca/certification/audit-and-certification-process/)。

## Recommended next steps for the farm
## 给农场的建议下一步

1. Treat CanadaGAP as not available for our shiitake production unless CanadaGAP changes its commodity scope.
   中文：除非 CanadaGAP 改变其商品范围，否则把 CanadaGAP 视为不适用于我们的香菇生产。
2. If a buyer asks for CanadaGAP, ask whether they require production certification specifically or whether a repacking/wholesale certificate would satisfy them. If they require production certification, CanadaGAP is the wrong scheme for shiitake.
   中文：如果买家要求 CanadaGAP，询问他们是否特别要求生产认证，或者再包装/批发证书是否可以满足要求。如果他们要求生产认证，CanadaGAP 对香菇来说不是正确方案。
3. Email CanadaGAP at `info@canadagap.ca` with our proposed operation description and ask for written scope guidance. Include: "Ontario indoor shiitake farm; growing/fruiting blocks; harvesting; first packing into clamshells/bulk cases; refrigerated storage; direct delivery; no purchased mushrooms" and ask whether any CanadaGAP option applies.
   中文：向 `info@canadagap.ca` 发送邮件，说明我们拟议的运营内容，并请求书面范围指导。包括："Ontario indoor shiitake farm; growing/fruiting blocks; harvesting; first packing into clamshells/bulk cases; refrigerated storage; direct delivery; no purchased mushrooms"，并询问是否有任何 CanadaGAP 选项适用。
4. Ask target buyers what alternate mushroom-appropriate food safety certification or audit they accept. Keep the buyer's written answer in this certification folder.
   中文：询问目标买家接受哪种适合蘑菇的替代食品安全认证或审核。将买家的书面答复保存在本 certification 文件夹中。
5. Regardless of certification route, build a mushroom-specific food safety program now using CanadaGAP-style controls: sanitation SOPs, harvest/pack records, lot codes, cold-chain logs, pest control, water tests if water contacts product or equipment, training records, visitor policy, recall procedure, and complaint/deviation logs.
   中文：无论选择哪种认证路径，现在都应使用 CanadaGAP 风格的控制措施建立蘑菇专属食品安全计划：卫生 SOP、采收/包装记录、批次代码、冷链记录、害虫控制、如水接触产品或设备则进行水质检测、培训记录、访客政策、召回程序，以及投诉/偏差记录。
