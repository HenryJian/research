# Repository Purpose

This repository is a working research base for operating a specialty mushroom farm in Waterford, Ontario, Canada.

Its main purpose is to:

- research topics that matter to the farm's operation,
- document findings and decisions,
- and preserve research results so they can be reused in future planning and execution.

# Farm Context

- The farm currently grows shiitake mushrooms.
- The farm is planning to grow oyster mushrooms in the near future.
- The farm is planning to grow maitake mushrooms in the near future.

# How To Interpret The Repository

Unless a document clearly says otherwise, assume the materials in this repository exist to support practical farm operations, business decisions, and long-term knowledge retention.

Examples:

- [distribution](/Users/hzjian/src/research/distribution) contains research related to how to sell and distribute the farm's products.
- [tax](/Users/hzjian/src/research/tax) contains research related to tax benefits and tax planning relevant to the farm.

# Guidance For Future Agents

- Treat this repository as an operational research archive, not just a general notes folder.
- When creating or updating documents, optimize for clarity, reuse, and decision support.
- Prefer research outputs that are actionable for a specialty mushroom farm in Ontario.
- Assume "we" refers to the farm operators unless the local document establishes a different perspective.
- When organizing new work, place it in topic-specific directories when possible so research results remain easy to find later.
